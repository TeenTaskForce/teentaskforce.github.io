Why we created TeenTaskForce

Teen Task Force is a business that allows people to hire teens in their neighborhood in order to complete household tasks they need help with. We are targeting teens who want to earn money and adults who need help. In our current world, teenagers usually turned away from work opportunities for several reasons: of their younger ages or are not ready for the commitment of an actual job, they usually do not receive the chance to earn money for themselves. Our service makes it possible for these teenagers to make some extra cash by assisting their neighbors. On the other hand, many adults are too busy or unable to finish certain chores or jobs at home, and our service again provides them with a solution to their problems. They can hire teenagers to complete their tasks. Additionally, most adults find teens immature and unprepared for the real world. After being hired through Teen Task Force, these teenagers will acquire important experiences in fields such as employee-employer interaction and managing money responsibly. In the future, our business has the potential to expand to a wider market, such as the whole Bay Area and additional cities in California.
